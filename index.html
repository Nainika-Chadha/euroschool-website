Achievements by Euroschool

Interschool science competition

We are what we repeatedly do. Excellence, then, is not an act, but a habit. Students of our school participated in Interschool Science Competition held at Pathfinders School Pataudi on 16th December. Two teams of our school participated in Science model making competition. The junior team and The senior team exhibited their models displaying methods for 'reducing pollution' and ideas of 'generating alternate resources of energy' as their initiatives for a better nature friendly life. They bagged the I and II position among number of participants. They proved that they are best orators with eloquent speaking skills in debate by winning two prize. Yet again our Senior and Junior team made us proud by winning First and second position. Our school team was declared the Best Team .
